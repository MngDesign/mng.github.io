About Me

Hello! I'm Murukan, a passionate UX designer with over 20 years of experience creating digital products that are both beautiful and functional. My background in user research and design allows me to approach problems from a unique perspective.

I believe in designing with empathy, understanding not just what users do, but why they do it. This human-centered approach has helped me create solutions that significantly improve user satisfaction and business metrics.

My career goal is to lead product design at a company that values innovation, user research, and creating meaningful experiences that make a positive impact on people's lives.
